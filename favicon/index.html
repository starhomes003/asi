As the largest open-sourced, independent entity in AI research and development, this alliance aims to accelerate the advancement of decentralized Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and, ultimately, Artificial Superintelligence (ASI).

The ASI Alliance represents a significant shift toward a decentralized AI ecosystem, offering an alternative to AI development dominated by big tech. By pooling their resources and expertise, Fetch.ai, SingularityNET, and Ocean Protocol aim to foster innovation, transparency, and inclusivity in the development of advanced AI technologies.

The founding team of the Artificial Superintelligence (ASI) Alliance is comprised of AI visionaries, technologists, and thought leaders from Fetch.ai, SingularityNET, and Ocean Protocol. They are at the forefront of decentralized, ethical, and safe AI development, pioneering advancements in Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and Artificial Superintelligence (ASI).
